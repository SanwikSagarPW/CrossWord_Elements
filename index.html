How to Play

Objective

Fill in the crossword grid with the correct words based on the given clues. Each word intersects with others, sharing common letters.

How to Play

  1. Select a Clue: Click on any clue from the ACROSS or DOWN lists to highlight the corresponding word in the grid.
  2. Enter Letters: Click on a cell in the grid and type the letter. The cursor will automatically move to the next cell.
  3. Navigate: Use arrow keys to move between cells, or click on any cell directly.
  4. Switch Direction: Click the same cell twice to toggle between ACROSS and DOWN directions.
  5. Check Progress: Click "Check Completion" to verify all cells are filled before submitting.
  6. Submit: Once all cells are filled, click "Submit Puzzle" to check your answers.

Scoring

Your score is based on completion time:

  • Under 4 minutes: 200 points
  • 4-6 minutes: 150 points
  • 6-8 minutes: 100 points
  • 8-10 minutes: 50 points

Game Features

  • Timer: 10-minute countdown displayed at the top
  • Word Highlighting: Selected words are highlighted in the grid
  • Clue Highlighting: Active clue is highlighted in the clue list
  • Visual Feedback: Correct answers turn green, incorrect ones flash red
